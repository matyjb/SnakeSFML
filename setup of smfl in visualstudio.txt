To hook up SFML.Net libraries right click your project in the solution explorer and select "Add Reference...". 
Then browse to the SFML.Net binding dlls under the "lib" folder that you downloaded. 
Now all you need is to add in the CSFML libraries. 
Now right click your solution and select "Add>>Existing Item...". 
Browse to the "extlibs" folder this time and add the unmanaged CSFML dlls as files to your project.
